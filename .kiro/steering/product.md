# Product Overview

This is a React Native mobile application for analyzing frisbee throws using pose detection (BlazePose). The app uses the device camera to capture photos and videos, likely for analyzing throwing form and technique.

## Key Features

- Camera capture with photo and video recording
- Media library integration for saving captures
- Video playback with controls
- Gesture-based camera controls (tap to capture, hold to record, pan to zoom)
- Cross-platform support (iOS, Android, Web)

## Target Platforms

- iOS (with camera permissions)
- Android (with camera and edge-to-edge display support)
- Web (limited functionality)
